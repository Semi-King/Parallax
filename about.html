About

Parallax scrolling relies on the principle of parallax, which is the apparent displacement or difference in the position of an object when viewed from different angles. In web design, this effect is achieved by moving multiple layers of content at different speeds as the user scrolls down the page.One of the main advantages of parallax scrolling is its ability to captivate users and provide a more dynamic browsing experience. By adding depth and movement to the page, it helps to draw attention, create visual interest, and enhance storytelling.
